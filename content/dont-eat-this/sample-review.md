## Quick Take

Great beer, solid pretzel, and a twenty-minute wait for a single high chair on a Tuesday. Bring snacks and manage expectations.

## The Review

The beer list is genuinely good and the pretzel is worth ordering twice. But this is clearly a brewery designed around adults lingering, not families moving through — seating is tight, there's exactly one high chair, and the patio (where you'd actually want to sit with a toddler) fills up fast. We'd go back, but on a weekday afternoon, not a Friday night.
