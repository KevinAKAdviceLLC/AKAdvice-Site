## Day 1

### Morning

Left home at 7am to beat traffic. Stopped for gas and coffee in Auburn — skip the drive-thru pastry case, it's overpriced for what you get.

### Afternoon

Checked into a budget motel near the lake ($120/night) and hit the public beach instead of a paid one. Free parking if you arrive before 11am.

### Evening

Picnic dinner from the grocery store instead of a restaurant — saved probably $60 over eating out with a toddler who wasn't going to sit still anyway.

## Lessons Learned / Tips

- Public beaches beat paid ones for a two-day trip with a toddler — nobody cares about the extra amenities at that age.
- Grocery store picnics save real money over two restaurant meals a day.
- Leave before the toddler's overtired window, not after.
