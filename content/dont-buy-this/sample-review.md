## Quick Take

We bought a battery-powered fan that clips onto a stroller so our toddler wouldn't melt on our afternoon walks. It works. It also eats batteries like it's personally offended by them.

## The Review

It clips on fine, it's quiet enough, and it does move air. The problem is battery life — we're talking two walks before it's dead, and it doesn't come with rechargeables. So the "cheap accessory" quietly turns into a recurring AA subscription. If you already have rechargeable batteries lying around, this is a fine impulse buy. If not, budget for that too.
