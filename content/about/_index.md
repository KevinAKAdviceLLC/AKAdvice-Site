We're a young family living in Northern California — faith-filled, budget-conscious, and unwilling to pretend a bad meal was good just because it was expensive.

AKAdvice is where we keep score: the products we bought, the restaurants we tried, and the trips we took. We're not chasing sponsorships and we're not trying to sell you anything. We just like writing it down, and figured someone else might find it useful before they spend their own money.

Fiscally responsible. Still having fun. That's the whole pitch.
